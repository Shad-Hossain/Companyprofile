About Us — Individual Company Details

Jamuna Logistics Limited

Type: Customs Clearing & Forwarding Agent

Handles customs clearance, import/export documentation, port coordination and freight forwarding with a focus on compliance and speed.

Mission: Reliable customs clearance and end-to-end forwarding solutions.
Vision: To be Bangladesh’s most trusted C&F partner.

JP Express Limited

Type: Customs Clearing & Forwarding Agent

Focuses on fast and secure shipments, express customs processing, last-mile coordination and reliable tracking.

Mission: Fast, accurate, and trustworthy express solutions.
Vision: To set benchmark standards in regional express services.

Upex World BD Limited

Type: International Courier & Freight

Connects Bangladesh to global markets via air & sea freight, express courier and e-commerce shipping solutions.

Mission: Seamless global connectivity for businesses and individuals.
Vision: To become Bangladesh’s leading international courier brand.

Type: Importer, Supplier & Developer

Supplies industrial equipment, consumer goods and supports development projects.

Mission: High-quality products and project delivery.
Vision: Trusted diversified business driving growth.
